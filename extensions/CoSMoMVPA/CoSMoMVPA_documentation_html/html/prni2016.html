PRNI 2016 workshop¶

CoSMoMVPA workshop at PRNI 2016, the 6th International Workshop on Pattern Recognition in Neuroimaging

presented by Nick Oosterhof

Location: FBK - POVO, Via Sommarive, 18, 38123 Trento. Room Sala consiglio (Tutorial 3).

(University of Trento students in the CIMeC 2016 hands-on method course, see CIMeC 2016 Hands-on Methods.)

History of exercises:

  • 2016 PRNI 2016, the 6th International Workshop on Pattern Recognition in Neuroimaging workshop.
  • 2016 CIMeC 2016 Hands-on Methods.
  • 2016 Royal Holloway University of London CoSMoMVPA workshop.
  • 2014 CIMeC 2014 Advanced Neural Decoding.
  • 2013 2013 Summer School in Computational Sensory-Motor Neuroscience.
